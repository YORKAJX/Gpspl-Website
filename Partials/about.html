About GPSPL

Complete IT & AV solution provider with nationwide service capability.

Global Peripheral Solution Pvt. Ltd. (GPSPL) is a New Delhi–based technology solutions company established in 1997. We specialise in the design, supply, installation, and support of integrated IT and Audio-Visual systems for enterprises, institutions, and government organisations.

Our portfolio covers everything from interactive displays, digital control rooms, LED video walls, and professional AV systems to enterprise servers, storage, networking, and power solutions.

In addition to sales and deployment, GPSPL provides AMC, on-site support, preventive maintenance, and warranty extension services to ensure long-term system reliability.
